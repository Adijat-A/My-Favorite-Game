My Favorite Game

Geometry dash is a rhythm game made by Robert Topala (RobTop) in 2013, over the following years, there have been a variety of updates with the most recent one being update 2.1! The GD community have been waiting for update 2.2 for about six years but in October of this year, update 2.2 will be released which has made alot of GD players excited for the following update such as Juniper, EVW and many more!



Achievements

Throughout the game there are about 266 achievements which can be rewarded by doing certain things, some being harder than others with the hardest one being to make a level good enough for GD Moderators to send them to RobTop and give a star rating. Due to there being so many updates, it's been harder to create a feature-worthy level as standards increase nearly every update.

Creating

Geometry Dash is a never-ending game due to the fact that you can create level that may or may not get a star rating. Because RobTop added a GD editor more and more levels have been added to the GD servers, some better than others. Having the dedication to create a wonderful masterpiece is a skill in itself as it can be very tiring to create such a high detail level such as this one . Although some levels are amazing, there is a limit towards how many objects you can put in a level so anyone on any device can play their levels with no problems but there's a bypass which allows you to go beyond the limit thus having a high object count (which is over 40k objects).

Demons

Demons are the hardest difficulty in the game. In the early days of Geometry Dash, everyone was on mobile which gave them a disadvantage making them nearly impossible to complete. At the time, it was easy for hackers to mess up the games servers as the game had a lot of insecurities and most demons got hacked for random purposes causing alot of distress in the GD community. A few updates later, Geometry Dash was added to PC and RobTop made the servers more secure so hackers can't cause anymore havoc among the servers and it was easier to slay demons as you could modify the FPS (Frames Per Second) which is allowed as some people may have lower end monitors.
